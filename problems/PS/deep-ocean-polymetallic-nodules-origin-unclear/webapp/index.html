Origin of Deep-Ocean Polymetallic Nodules

Abiotic vs Biogenic formation: quantifying pathway contributions

Key Finding

Nodule formation involves mixed pathways: Monte Carlo analysis (n=500) estimates 0.115 hydrogenetic, 0.573 diagenetic, and 0.312 biogenic contributions. Abiotic processes dominate in 84.6% of parameter space, but the 27-31% biogenic contribution is significant and measurable through iron isotope signatures (Cohen's d = 5.789).
